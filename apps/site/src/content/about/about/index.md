This site collects selected works, open-ended experiments, reading notes, and a few small coordinates about the person behind them.

The pace here is intentionally slow. Content is written in Markdown, versioned in Git, and published as a static site so the structure stays light and durable.

Design decisions lean toward clarity, quiet contrast, and enough warmth to keep the site from feeling sterile.
